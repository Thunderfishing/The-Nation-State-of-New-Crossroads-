## Purpose
Establishes a network of **5,000 member-owned credit unions** (250 per region across 20 regions) that act as New Crossroads’ decentralized financial backbone.  

Credit unions issue and manage Bulwark Coin (BWC), deliver reserve-backed Wallets that grow the national metals reserve, provide loans ($452.5 billion in 2025 → $1.2084 trillion by 2075), banking suites ($208 billion), and shares ($94 billion), while allowing every citizen-member to build personal wealth through low-barrier entry, tiered incentives, and ETF-driven reserve growth.  

They self-recapitalize via fees, interest, and SWF/SMSWF profits, support the 65% co-op / 15% corporate / 20% informal GDP split, and ensure broad economic inclusion.

## Section 1: Core Features & Currency

### 1.1 Dual Currency Role
- **Physical Cash** ($362.5 billion, 2.5% GDP): Gold/silver-flecked polymer notes, heavily used in informal sector (<$100/BWC transactions). 1% swap fee to BWC ($362.5 million/year).  
- **Bulwark Coin (BWC)**: Blockchain-based, backed by 0.025 oz precious metal reserve per BWC (\~$50/BWC in 2025). Basket: 30% gold, 30% palladium, 15% platinum, 10% iridium, 10% rhodium, 5% ruthenium. Scales to \~$300/BWC backing by 2075 via ETF returns.  

### 1.2 Self-Recapitalization & Reserve Growth
Credit unions **self-recapitalize** through:  
- Transaction, swap, checking, liquidity, micro-loan, and insurance fees  
- Interest on loans (micro 4%, non-SWF 5–6%)  
- SMSWF profits ($2.48 billion/year → $1.736 billion to credit unions)  
- Reserve ETF & SWF investment returns  

Every citizen who buys into a Wallet **grows the national metals reserve** via ETF investments (BWC Reserve ETF + SMSWF ETF), which allocate returns to base backing until \~$300/BWC target, then to personal savings.

### 3.1 Base Shares
- **Cost**: $1/BWC, max 1,000 ($1,000/BWC lifetime, $94 billion system-wide).

### 3.2 Special Shares (Citizen-Members Only)

### 3.3 Reserve-Backed Wallet – How It Grows the National Reserve
- **Base Backing**: Starts at 0.025 oz (\~$50/BWC).  
- **ETF Mechanism** (BWC Reserve ETF 3–5% + SMSWF ETF 5–7%):  
  - Returns first allocate to increase base backing (target \~$300/BWC over decades).  
  - Once target reached, excess flows to Member Savings Account (personal use).  
- **Citizen Contribution**: Invest up to $5,000–$10,000/BWC per ETF → directly adds physical metals to national reserve via ETF holdings.  
- **Scaling**: From $15.04 billion Wallets (2025) → $16.25 billion+ by 2075; ETF growth pushes reserve from $125–$175 billion → $407–$504 billion.
